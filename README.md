# Car Insurance Risk & Profitability Analytics

This project is part of my work at AlphaCare Insurance Solutions to analyze historical car insurance claims data in South Africa and develop predictive models for risk assessment and marketing strategy optimization.

## Data Source

Historical data (Feb 2014 – Aug 2015) from AlphaCare internal records.

## Project Structure

- `data/`: Raw and processed datasets
- `notebooks/`: EDA and modeling notebooks
- `scripts/`: Helper scripts for analysis
- `plots/`: Visualization outputs

## Tools Used

- Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
- Scikit-learn, Statsmodels
- Git, GitHub, DVC

Task 3: Hypothesis Testing
Objective
Statistically determine whether certain factors (e.g., Province, ZipCode, Gender) significantly influence risk or profit metrics — such as:

Claim Frequency (how often claims occur),

Claim Severity (how big claims are),

Margin (TotalPremium - TotalClaims).
